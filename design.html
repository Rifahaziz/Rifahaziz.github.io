The Duality in Space and Personality:

PROJECT 1:
Designed a space for the leopard and the seal to co-exist peacefully, with the help of the moving gate, they can choose to stay with/without each other's company.


The Listening Hill:

PROJECT 2:
Observing a space (a hill located at Mooney's Bay at Ottawa), and the activities that are carried out depending on the time of the day! The humans are handrawn and then digitalized with Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop

The Shell Cities :

PROJECT 3:
Inspired by Calvino's twin cities, I came up with the idea of designing mirroring cities, where both cities will be a mirror of their own yet different. he whole city is inspired by beach elements.Thought of symmetrical objects. A unique and imaginary method of travelling between the cities. Umbrella it was! Inspired bythe symmetry and round polygonal shapes of an umbrella, I thought of how it could be used asa means of travelling. The buildings shapes are inspired from a sea shell interior, connecting the roundness of the sea shells with umbrellas.
